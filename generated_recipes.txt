Recipe Name: Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 1 lb beef, thinly sliced
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 1 cup rice
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
2. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
3. Add beef slices and cook until browned, then remove from skillet and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, add garlic and ginger, saut� for 1 minute.
5. Add broccoli florets and cook until tender-crisp.
6. Return beef to the skillet, pour in soy sauce, and stir to combine.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
8. Serve the beef and broccoli stir-fry over cooked rice. Enjoy!

Recipe Name: Sweet and Sour Pork Stir Fry

Ingredients:
- 1 lb pork, thinly sliced
- 1 cup pineapple chunks
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 cup rice
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, minced
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook rice according to package instructions.

6. Stir in bell peppers and pineapple chunks, cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Pour the sauce mixture over the pork and vegetables, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
9. Serve the sweet and sour pork stir fry over cooked rice. Enjoy!

Recipe Name: Coconut Curry Chicken with Rice

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, cut into cubes
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
- 1 cup rice
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup chicken broth
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
1. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and bell pepper. Cook until softened.
2. Add chicken cubes and cook until browned on all sides.
3. Sprinkle curry powder over the chicken and vegetables. Stir to coat evenly.
4. Pour in coconut milk and chicken broth. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
5. Cover and let it simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
6. In a separate pot, cook rice according to package instructions.
7. Serve the coconut curry chicken over rice. Garnish with fresh cilantro. Enjoy!

Recipe Name: Homemade Hummus

Ingredients:
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:
1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, garlic, tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, cumin, and salt.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
3. If the hummus is too thick, add a little water or more olive oil until desired consistency is reached.
4. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
5. Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl and drizzle with extra olive oil, sprinkle with cumin, and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
6. Serve with pita bread, vegetables, or use as a spread or dip. Enjoy!

Recipe Name: Lemon Dill Salmon with Asparagus

Ingredients:
- 4 salmon fillets
- 1 lemon (juiced and zested)
- 2 tablespoons fresh dill (chopped)
- 4 cloves garlic (minced)
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 bunch asparagus
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400�F (200�C).
2. In a small bowl, mix together lemon juice, lemon zest, dill, garlic, and 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
3. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
4. Brush the lemon dill mixture over the salmon fillets.
5. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
6. Bake the salmon in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until cooked through.
7. While the salmon is baking, trim the ends of the asparagus and toss them with the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil, salt, and pepper.
8. Place the asparagus on a separate baking sheet and roast in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until tender.
9. Serve the lemon dill salmon with roasted asparagus on the side. Enjoy!

- 1 packet yeast
- 1 1/4 cups warm water
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, yeast, and salt.
2. Gradually add warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients, mixing until a dough forms.
3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour.
5. Preheat the oven to 400�F (200�C).
6. Punch down the risen dough and shape it into a loaf.
7. Place the loaf on a baking sheet, cover with a cloth, and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
8. Bake the bread in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.
9. Let the bread cool before slicing and serving. Enjoy your homemade bread!

Recipe Name: Chocolate Vanilla Custard

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cream
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 200g dark chocolate, chopped

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 325�F (165�C).
2. In a saucepan, heat the cream and sugar over medium heat until it starts to simmer. Remove from heat.
3. In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs and vanilla extract together.
